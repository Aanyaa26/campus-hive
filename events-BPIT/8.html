FITT-IITD-FCI Hackathon

FITT-IIT Delhi in association with Food Corporation of India(FCI) is inviting startups, individuals, and teams of innovators to solve the challenge related to supply chain, Logistics management and product design. FITT-IITD<>FCI Hackathon aims to support innovators to grow from the ideation stage to developing workable prototypes and minimum viable products for the use cases. Problem statement : Design an independent mechanical portable system to provide loading/unloading of foodgrains packed in gunny bags Prototyping grant up to 10 lakhs!
