Crossword Expedition

National Inter-College Crossword Expedition (NICE-23) jointly organizing the event with National Institute of Industrial Engineering (NITIE), Mumbai. A contest involving solving of cryptic crossword clues. is scheduled to be held between March-August 2023 and registration has started on March 2023. The contest will be conducted through both online (initial rounds) and offline modes (zonal and final rounds).  The whole exercise, right from registration of participants and execution of the three rounds is to be anchored through Extra-C AICTE invites and encourages all affiliated institutions to take part in National Inter College Crossword Expedition 2023 (NICE-23).
