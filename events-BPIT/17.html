Udyaan Utsav

Greetings from All India Council for Technical Education ! As you are aware, the Gardens in Rashtrapati Bhavan are open to the public for about a month during February- March every year. To encourage and enable more people to visit and enjoy the beauty of rare varieties of flowers and plants in these gardens, it has been decided this year to open the Gardens of Rashtrapati Bhavan for two months, ie. from 31s January to 31st March 2023, under the outreach programme, "Udyan Utsav. 2023". For any further details/ information in the matter your office may contact the undersigned on Mobile No. 9205855997 and/or E-mail ID :- udyanutsav-rb@rb.nic.in Please find attached O.M and Instructions for your kind perusal. In view of the above, AICTE requests your Institution(s) to kindly disseminate this information among all the faculty members, Students, staff & others so they can take the benefits from this Udyan Utsav.
