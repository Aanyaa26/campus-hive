Greetings from IEEE UVCE! CodeStorm When life throws you a CodeStorm, pass all the test cases! CodeStorm is a competitive programming contest. Here, the participants are required to solve a series of problems over 2 rounds. These problems would challenge their problem solving skills and promote out-of-the box thinking. Round 1: Here a set of 5 problems would be given, each of varying degrees of difficulty. The problem solving skills and knowledge of the participants will be tested. Round 2: The qualifying contestants will make it to round two. The participants are required to solve a fun optimisation problem statement, and maximize their score. Cash Prize worth ₹6000 to be won!
