Metaverse Program

The 'Creators of Metaverse' program allows students to learn about AR & VR and gain hands-on experience with industry-grade AR software in developing immersive solutions. It is a three phase workshop. Phase 1: The first phase is a 2-day workshop consisting of 2-hour sessions on Metaverse 101 and creating your first AR effect. Phase 2: The second phase is a 1-month micro-internship program with project-based knowledge checkpoints, live lectures, and mentor sessions. Phase 3: The final phase is the formation of alumni of the micro-internship cohorts into the 'Creators of Metaverse' community in chapters across the country, providing events and industry showcase opportunities.
